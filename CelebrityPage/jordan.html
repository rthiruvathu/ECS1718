Michael Jordan

Michael Jordan is dubbed the G.O.A.T. of basketball.

Information

Michael Jeffrey Jordan is a former basketball player and currently a businessman. He owns the Charlotte Bobcats in NBA. He played 15 seasons for both the Washington Wizards and the Chicago Bulls. With the Chicago Bulls he won 6 NBA championships. He won the NBA championships in 1991-1993 and 1996-1998. Jordan is widely known for his famous free throw line dunk which happened during a few NBA Dunk Contests. He also owns the Air Jordan company which makes basketball shoes and athletic apparel. He is the first ever billionare basketball player.

Awards

  • 6x NBA Champion
  • 5x MVP
  • 6x NBA Finals MVP
  • NBA Defensive Player of the Year 1987-1988

What MJ does now

  1. Owns the Company Air Jordan
  2. Owns the Charlotte Bobcats
  3. Is a spokesman for many companies
